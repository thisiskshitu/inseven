InSeven

Number Guessing in 7 steps

Welcome to InSeven. Choose a number from 1 to 100 (inclusive) and the computer will guess your number in a maximum of 7 steps

At each step computer will ask you whether your number was this and you have to tell the computer whether his guess was low or high

For example if your number is 14 and the computer asked whether your number was 50 then press the high button to tell the computer that his guess was higher than your number and vice versa if for example your number was 70 and the computer guess was 50 then press the low button to tell the computer that his guess was lower than your number

Press the button Yes! when the computer has guessed your number correctly

The steps counter is always present below the buttons to show how many steps the computer took to guess your number
